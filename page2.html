I Remember

I remember the fragrance of damp air,
I remember an olive tree gleaming on the earth.
I remember olives harmonizing with cookies,
I remember the glass resembling an inverted stream.

I remember delicately wiping dust off the glass,
I remember the soprano serenading from afar.
I remember her bells, gently given a shake,
I remember your hand, tenderly embracing the ring box, without a ring.

I remember the expired milk, a bittersweet taste,
I remember the aroma of your essence carried by the wind, entwining with mine.
